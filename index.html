free (as in free beer)

The modern minimal glyphs are free, even for commercial use. However, if you still wish to make a donation towards the continued development of the icons, feel free to do so. Thank you!

infinitely scalable

Composed of vector shapes, the glyphs are scalable to any size - which means you can have them as big or as small as you want. No blurry edges, no fuzzy pixels.

at home anywhere

The open forms and minimal styling make the modern minimal glyphs well suited for a variety of tasks. On the web, mobile applications, and even in print, the modern minimal family will always look sharp.

100% royalty free

The modern minimal set is free for you to use, re-use, and share with others. Licensed under the Creative Commons, you are free use this family of glyphs however you see fit - except, of course, selling them as your own work. Just be sure to tell people where you got 'em. Easy.
